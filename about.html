About Properties

A residential property is a type of real estate that is designed and intended for living purposes. It can take many forms, from a single-family home to an apartment building with multiple units. Residential properties are typically located in neighborhoods or communities that offer a range of amenities and services to residents, such as parks, schools, shopping centers, and public transportation.

Commercial property refers to real estate that is intended for business purposes, rather than residential use. It can include a wide variety of properties, such as office buildings, retail stores, warehouses, restaurants, hotels, and more.

A plot of land is a piece of real estate that is typically undeveloped and can be used for a variety of purposes, such as residential, commercial, agricultural, or industrial. A plot of land can vary in size, shape, and location, and can be found in rural, suburban, or urban areas.
